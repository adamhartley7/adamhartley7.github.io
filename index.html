CheckpointKM

Proof that the number in the paper came from the code.

It re-runs a published analysis in a frozen environment, then a second, independent model adversarially verifies every reported number against the code that produced it — on two axes: does it follow from the code, and is the method sound. It's allowed to say “I can't confirm this.”
