what makes TOP different

A normal usage screen is a receipt. TOP aims to give you the quote first.

AI companies publish prices for small pieces of text. That does not tell you the price of finishing a real task. The AI may need several attempts, use other software or continue the conversation. TOP is testing whether your previous work can make the next estimate more useful.

Think of a taxi with no meter. AI work can feel like getting in without knowing the fare. TOP aims to show a sensible range before the journey starts.

one system, three layers

Know the cost. Choose the right AI. Improve the work.

TOP stands for Token Optimization Protocol, meaning a system for getting more useful work from your AI allowance. Tokens are the small pieces of text AI services count when measuring usage. TOP-1 is what we are building now. TOP-2 and TOP-3 are ideas for later. Icarus, Daedalus and Athena are temporary names.

TOP-1 · Icarus · being tested

Know before you fly.

Icarus is the warning.

In the myth, Icarus ignored the safe limit and flew too close to the sun. TOP-1 aims to show a low, likely and high cost before an AI task begins.

Like a weather forecast for an AI task. It shows a likely range, not a promise of the exact bill.
Works today: a report about past usage. Being tested: the next-task estimate. We have not yet shown the complete estimate working reliably in a public test.
TOP-2 · Daedalus · idea for later

Choose a smarter route.

Daedalus is the inventor.

Daedalus built the wings and understood the safe way to fly. TOP-2 is an idea for comparing AI versions and finding the right balance of cost and quality for a task.

Like sat-nav choosing the route, not merely showing the fuel price.
Not available yet. We have not proved that it saves money.
TOP-3 · Athena · idea for later

Learn to work smarter.

Athena is the strategist.

Athena represents wisdom and strategy. TOP-3 is planned as a personal coach that asks useful questions, learns how you work and helps turn a rough request into a better plan.

Like a coach who knows how you work, not the same set of instructions for everyone.
Not available yet. We have not proved that it improves anyone's work.
why it matters

The advertised AI price is not the price of finishing your task.

A price list for bricks cannot tell you what a whole house will cost. An AI price list has the same problem.

01

One task can hide many steps

A short request can lead to planning, extra software, repeated attempts and a long conversation. The advertised price does not reveal the final bill.

02

Your habits change the cost

Two people can ask for the same result and use AI very differently. A useful estimate should learn from how your work actually runs.

03

The lesson arrives too late

A bill is a rear-view mirror. By the time it shows an expensive task, the money and time have already been spent.

the TOP-1 goal

From one task description to a sensible range.

This is how Icarus is intended to work. The report available today does not estimate your next task yet.

01

Describe the task

Say what you want in everyday words, such as fixing a sign-in bug, researching a market or drafting a report.

02

Compare it with the past

TOP aims to find similar completed tasks and use your own history when enough useful history exists.

03

See the range and decide

A low, likely and high figure should help you run the task, change the approach or wait. It is an estimate, not a guarantee.
